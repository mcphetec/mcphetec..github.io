Target Audience

My primary target audience for this website is older people who are looking to retire or go on vacation for a family reunion.

The secondary target audience is mother's of families who have children. Let's be real, mom's do the planning for trips the most, especially in Utah and Idaho.

Personas and Scenarios

Persona 1 - Deborah

Scenario 1

My name is Deborah and I am having a big family reunion in Soda Springs, ID. We do have some concerns though about the weather beause it's January. I need weather updates up until the day before we get there to make sure that it's safe for my family that's going to be driving in.

Scenario 2

Hey! It's Deborah again, we were so excited with our trip to Fish Haven, ID that we actually want to retire there! We do want to know what we can expect with the weather though so we want to be able to watch it this winter and take notes of how bad the snow really can be. I need to have a website that I can easily navigate to and watch what the weather is like there! Do you know of one?


Persona 2 - Michelle

Scenario 3

Scenario 4

It's Michelle again and I have great news! We added another kid to the family! My husband and I need a break so we can retain what sanity we have left though. Do you know the weather in Soda Springs, ID tomorrow? We have got to get out of this house!

Guess who?! It's Michelle. Our kids are grown up now and like teenagers they want to take the car and some friends up to Preston, ID.
